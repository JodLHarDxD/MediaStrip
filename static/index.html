— ABOUT

Move media.

MediaStrip is a local-first content toolkit for creators, editors, and researchers who refuse to wait on cloud queues or compromise on quality.

Pull content from a thousand sources at native resolution — no re-encodes, no lossy passes. Strip watermarks frame-by-frame with a GPU-accelerated diffusion pipeline that actually understands what it's looking at.
